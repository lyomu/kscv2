ABOUT US

Overview
Kenya Seed Company (KSC) Limited is a State Corporation established under the Companies Act of 2015 and is governed by the State Corporations Act Cap 446 among other Acts of Parliament. The mandate of KSC is to carry out focused research, promote and facilitate production of high yielding, better quality certified seed varieties suitable for various agro-ecological zones for enhanced food sufficiency, sustainable economic development, and quality living standards. The Company has diverse seed varieties of maize, wheat, sunflower, finger millet, rice, sorghum, legumes and pasture, indigenous vegetables, and horticultural crops. The Company was initially established by a group of farmers as a private company in July 1956 to promote the use of improved strains of pasture seed developed by the then, National Agricultural Research Station based in Kitale, which was later converted to a public liability company in 1960. In 1963, maize production was introduced following the release of the first hybrid by the Government Research Centre in Kitale. Wheat was introduced in 1971 to provide certified wheat seed to farmers who previously depended on low quality farm-saved seeds. Since those formative years, the Company has made substantial contribution towards food security through the development, production and provision of high yielding varieties and quality seeds of various crops adaptable to different agro-ecological zones. The Company has since diversified into other products and has over 60 seed varieties of maize, wheat, sunflower, finger millet, rice, sorghum, legumes, pasture, indigenous vegetables and horticultural seeds.
As an expansion strategy, KSC has four wholly owned subsidiaries namely:
1. Simlaw Seeds, Kenya
2. Kibo Seed, Tanzania
3. Simlaw Seeds, Uganda
4. Kenya Seed, Rwanda

Our Core Values
KSC will be guided by the following core values:
Customer focus
We shall deliver value and build long-term relationships with our customers by actively listening to their feedback and continuously improving on our products and services.
Integrity
We shall execute our mandate with honesty and transparency, and our conduct will be guided by the highest level of professionalism and ethical standards.
Teamwork
We shall foster a collaborative and supportive work environment where individuals work cohesively towards a common goal, promoting a sense of belonging and pride among the workforce.

We shall foster an environment where new ideas are encouraged and employees are empowered to think unconventionally.
